## 切线法线

- 切线斜率 * 法线斜率 = -1

$$
\begin{cases}
  f_{( x_0 )}^\prime * k = -1
  \\ \\
  y - y_0 = k ( x - x_0 )
\end{cases}
$$

## 曲面法线

$$ 设曲线方程: z = f_{( x, y )} = 0 $$

- 点(x0, y0)处的法线

$$
\dfrac{x - x_0}{\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} \mid_{x = x_0}} = \dfrac{y - y_0}{\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} \mid_{y = y_0}}
$$
