## 点到点

$$
\begin{cases}
  点A: ( x_0, y_0 )
  \\ \\
  点B: ( x_1, y_1 )
  \\ \\
  \mid AB \mid = \sqrt{( x_1 - x_0 )^2 + ( y_1 - y_0 )^2}
\end{cases}
$$

## 点到线

$$
\begin{cases}
  点A: ( x_0, x_1 )
  \\ \\
  线l: \; Ax + By + C = 0
  \\ \\
  d = \dfrac{\mid A x_0 + B y_0 + C \mid}{\sqrt{A^2 + B^2}}
\end{cases}
$$
